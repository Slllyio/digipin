Analysis

Network modification: 15 reverse edges removed in the old city area (1km radius from 24.642N, 77.310E), converting bidirectional roads to one-way.

Moderate demand: Minimal impact — travel time increases less than 1%, confirming the one-way conversion does not significantly penalize drivers.

Peak demand: Waiting time drops significantly while travel times remain stable, demonstrating the capacity benefit of eliminating head-on conflicts.

Recommendation: One-way conversion is viable. Implement as time-restricted one-way during peak hours initially.
